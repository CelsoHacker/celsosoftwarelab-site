Privacy

Your files stay on your machine

🛡️

Offline by default

OCR runs locally on your PC. Your documents are never uploaded or sent anywhere. No external server is contacted unless you explicitly enable online translation mode.

⚠️ Optional online translation mode may send extracted text to the selected service only when you turn it on. Offline mode never connects to the internet.
